AG1 SERIES

The AG1 Series is designed specifically for agricultural applications related to poultry, swine, turkey, equine and other controlled animal envirorunents. The Re-Verber-Ray AG1 Series is a complete product line of single stage, tube-type infra-red heaters that will offer standard control of a typical heated space in an agricultural operation.

DETAILS / SPECIFICATIONS:

Product Literature:
Re-Verber-Ray Infra-Red Heating Systems Designed For Agricultural Applications = Enhanced Overall Animal Performance + Energy Savings Up To 35%
Re-Verber-Ray AG1 Series single stage tube-type infra-red heaters offer standard control of a typical heated space in an agricultural operation related to poultry, swine, turkey, equine and other controlled animal environments.
Overall Design Criteria

For all agricultural applications we recommend that a qualified person (such as an agricultural engineer or factory trained design specialist) complete a detailed environmental analysis to ensure proper heater selection and overall system compatibility. Re-Verber-Ray infra-red heating systems are an excellent method of optimizing the animals environmental needs.
