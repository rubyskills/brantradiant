MP Series

Modulating Plus Tube Heater

The MP Series fully modulating infrared heater is the only infrared heater that allows you to select the desired performance curve that best suits your building requirements.

The MP Series by Brant Radiant Heaters Limited raises the bar with modulating technology. This full air and gas modulation line of heaters incorporates cutting edge technology such as the selection of three different performance curves as well as a ‘Blast Mode’™ feature for extreme environmental demands.

 

The MP Series “Modulating Plus” Overhead Infrared Heater is a finalist in Plant Engineering Magazine’s 2012 Product of the Year contest. Our entry is featured in the November issue of Plant Engineering in the HVAC category.

Features:

  • ‘Blast Mode’™ feature allows the user to temporarily lock the heater into its highest input plus an extra 5,000 BTU’s for extreme environmental demands.
  • Choose from three performance modes.
  • Models range from 80,000 to 200,000 BTU’s.
  • Full gas modulation with corresponding air flow modulation to achieve optimal fuel utilization and comfort levels.
  • Forced high-fire start up for faster response to the environmental demand.
  • Full proportional-integral-derivative (PID) logic to reduce on/off cycles over traditional heat.

Specifications:

  • Input Range: 80,000 to 200,000 BTU/h
  • Lengths: 25-70 Ft.
  • Gas Type: Natural or Propane
  • Combustion Chamber: Aluminized or Titanium Stabilized Steel
  • Radiant Tubes: Aluminized Steel
  • Certification: CSA Design Certified, ANSI Z83.20b
  • Limited Warranty: 1-5-10 (Components-Tubes-Burner)
