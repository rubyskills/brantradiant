AG1 Series

AGRICULTURAL GAS-FIRED, SINGLE-STAGE INFRARED HEATERS

The AG1 Series is designed for poultry and non-corrosive agricuttural environments. Proven construction features assure reliable performance at an economical price. Performance and flame distribution are enhanced by the use of a stainless steel 'swirr' burner. Radiant output is optimized with highly polished aluminum reflectors. Standard features include single stage operation, hot surface ignition, indicator lights, most mounting accessories and potter (waterproof) Circuit boards. For use in poultry, dairy, arena, barns, shops and much more.
