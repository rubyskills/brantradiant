PT Series

High Intensity Portable Infrared Construction Heaters

Portable infrared construction heaters are high intensity heaters that mount atop a LP tank and are an ideal solution when heat is temporarily needed. PT Series heaters are perfect for buildings under construction, alteration, or repair.

Available in either 16,000 or 32,000 BTU/h.

These units include: A patented safety tip over device for extra safety, mounting bracket, LP hose, and regulator.
