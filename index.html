Compare the Comfort

Traditional hot air heating systems waste energy by blowing warm air downward, which then rises again to the ceiling of a building, where it is wasted. Re-Verber-Ray and Quartz-Ray heaters quietly focus energy efficient infra-red rays to the floor area, thus maintaining a clean, even heat. In drafty applications, such as loading areas, infra-red heating can reduce heat recovery time by supplying a continuous "blanket" of infra-red heat energy to the selected floor area. The absorbed radiant heat energy is less affected by multiple air exchanges that occur in many work environments.
