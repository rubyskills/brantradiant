LS3 Series

Single-Stage, Residentially Approved* Infrared Tube Heater

Packed with quality features, the LS3 Series represents the finest in single-stage low BTU heaters.

Designed for application into low ceiling or select residential applications, LS3 Series heaters are the lowest BTU output heaters offered by Re-Verber-Ray. Performance is enhanced through the use of a specially designed combustion burner, titanium combustion chamber and black coated, aluminized steel radiant tubes. The LS3 Series is ideal for garages, patios, solariums, workshops, kennels, verandahs and other similar type applications.

* Select models are CSA Design Certified for use in residential applications (attached to living quarters).

Features:

  • Hot Surface Ignition with Flame Rod Sensing.
  • External operational light(s).
  • 24V Control Wiring with Micro-processor Circuitry.
  • Dimple Baffle-MAX technology.
  • Tool-free access to controls.
  • Separated air and gas burner chamber.
  • Symmetrical placementof ignition tube.
  • 3-piece independent compartment cover.
  • ABS plastic air intake collar for connection to 4" PVC/DWV pipe.
