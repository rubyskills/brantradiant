LS Series

Single-Stage, Residentially Approved* Infrared Tube Heater

Packed with quality features, the LS Series represents the finest in single-stage low BTU heaters.

Designed for application into low ceiling or select residential applications, LS Series heaters are the lowest BTU output heaters offered by Re-Verber-Ray. Performance is enhanced through the use of a specially designed combustion burner, titanium combustion chamber and black coated, aluminized steel radiant tubes. The LS Series is ideal for garages, patios, solariums, workshops, kennels, verandahs and other similar type applications.

* Select models are CSA Design Certified for use in residential applications (attached to living quarters).
