AVS Series

Single-Stage, Agricultural Infrared Quad Tube Heaters

The world's first low intensity brooder designed specifically for the poultry industry!

Patent pending technology combines the operating benefits of both high and low intensity infrared into a multi-tube, multi-burner, single exhaust appliance.

The AVS Series unique design provides superior performance and is ideal for applications seeking even heat intensity.

Construction features include: Dual spark ignition, highly emissive heat exchangers, polished reflectors, operational indicator lights, compact modular design and more!

Operation benefits include: Proven reduced energy consumption characteristics, spot heating functionality, high BTU output, low electrical consumption, combustion air option, direct vent option and the unit is not susceptible to high winds.
