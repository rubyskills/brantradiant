About Us

Brant Radiant Heaters Limited

has manufactured the Re-Verber-Ray and Quartz-Ray infra-red products in Canada for over 40 years. Realizing the need for dependable energy-efficient natural gas, propane and electric infra-red heating systems we are proud to offer the finest equipment available. Our overall market scope includes infra-red heating for new and retrofit applications in agriculture, residential and industrial/commercial as well as the design and manufacture of infra-red process burners for original equipment manufacturers.

If environmentally-sensitive energy conservation is a part of your plans, you owe it to your bottom line to investigate the natural alternative -- infra-red heating. From all the people at Brant Radiant, our network of Canadian distributors and associates, we look forward to hearing from you.

Brant Radiant Heaters Limited

is "Canada's leader in infra-red technology." Extensive in-house research, coupled with field-proven results over three decades, has enabled our company to continue to expand through innovative technologies that increase the applications for Re-Verber-Ray gas and Quartz-Ray electric infra-red heaters.

Our continued industry leadership includes an in-house quality control system that performance tests each heater, and a "blueprint" of every single valve- train, before leaving our ultra-modem Canadian manufacturing­facility in Paris, Ontario.

Through our network of factory-trained and authorized distributors across Canada, Brant Radiant Heaters provides complete inspection and evaluation services for either new or retrofitted industrial, commercial and agricultural applications.
